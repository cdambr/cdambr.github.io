Chiara Pia D'Ambrosio

PhD Candidate in Strategy & Entrepreneurship
London Business School

I study how organizations strategically use language and direct attention to navigate competing demands, with a particular interest in ESG communication. I work under the supervision of Professor Dónal Crilly.

Prior to joining LBS, I worked as a research assistant at SDA Bocconi School of Management and as a financial controller at Luxottica Group. I hold a BSc in Finance and an MSc in Accounting, Financial Management and Control, both from Bocconi University.
