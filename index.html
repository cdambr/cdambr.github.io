I am a PhD candidate in Strategy and Entrepreneurship at London Business School. My research explores how organizations communicate under uncertainty, with a focus on temporal framing and audience responses. Prior to joining LBS, I worked as a research assistant at SDA Bocconi School of Management and as a controller at Luxottica Group. I hold a BSc in Finance and an MSc in Accounting, Financial Management and Control, both from Bocconi University.

Working Papers

Temporal Framing and Audience Responses Under Uncertainty
Chiara Pia D'Ambrosio
Academy of Management Proceedings, 2025

This paper examines how organizations use temporal perspectivization strategies to frame future-oriented communications and how heterogeneous audiences respond to these strategies under conditions of uncertainty.
